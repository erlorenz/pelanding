How It Works

Schedule your order

Simply create your order by walking through our interactive web app. Schedule your pickup and return, select the items you'll be giving us, and finalize your order in a few easy steps.

Leave your garments

Leave your garments at the pickup location before the scheduled time so they are ready for us to grab.

Let us do the rest

We do the rest! We will have it freshly pressed and returned by the selected return time at the same location. You will be updated via text each step of the way!

Our Services

Press Express will refresh your clothing and return your garments professionally finished, while eliminating 90% of odors, smoke, etc.

In addition we can carry out minor repairs if requested, such as resewing buttons or resealing hems.

Finally, we do all this with the quickest turnaround in the city, making sure you have your garments back either same day or the following morning.

Pickup and delivery is always included in the price and we aim to make everything as simple as possible with our step-by-step online ordering.

We do not dry clean garments, they are steam-pressed and hand-finished.

About Us

Press Express was founded in 2018 as a new concept with the goal of providing a much needed service for business travelers, groups, as well as anyone looking for a quick and easy way to get their garments looking good in Las Vegas.

We realized that many people don't need their clothing dry cleaned or washed, but simply pressed free of wrinkles and creases so they look sharp for events, business conferences, conventions, or anything else.

Although this business is young, the owners have over 30 years of experience in the industry and the know-how to provide expert and professional service. Our location only 1/2 a mile from The Strip and less than 1 mile from Downtown allows us to pickup and return garments faster than any other service in the city.
